Co-organized the Solar Eclipse Bike Campout with Komorebi Cycling - a bike ride and campout from Portland to Salem, OR to celebrate the total solar eclipse on August 21st, 2017. In total we had 44 riders from Seattle, Los Angeles, New Orleans, Detroit, Denver and Portland. As promotion for the event I created the website, shot photography, designed a special iron-on patch and created posters.
